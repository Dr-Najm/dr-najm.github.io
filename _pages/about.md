I am a Senior Lecturer at the Faculty of Manufacturing & Mechatronic Engineering Technology, Universiti Malaysia Pahang Al-Sultan Abdullah (UMPSA), Pekan Campus. My research focuses on terrain-adaptive robot mobility, soft-soil terramechanics, and grouser-assisted locomotion. I also work on duct inspection robotics and supervise postgraduate research in AI-powered robot navigation.
