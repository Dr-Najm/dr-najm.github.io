projects

Research and engineering projects in robotics, mechatronics, and control systems.

robotics

HVAC Duct Cleaning & Inspection Robotic System

A full-stack mechatronic system for automated cleaning and inspection of HVAC duct networks, integrating active brush mechanisms, embedded sensing, tethered power delivery, and real-time vision.

Grouser-Based Mobility Enhancement for Rugged Terrain Robots

Experimental and design work on grouser-integrated track systems to improve traction, slip reduction, and load distribution for field robots operating in challenging terrain.

control systems

Self-Balancing Robot Platform for Control Systems Research

An Arduino-based inverted pendulum platform serving as a continuously evolving testbed for PID control development, stability analysis, and undergraduate control systems education.

agricultural robotics

Agricultural Pest Deterrent Robot

An autonomous mobile robot designed for non-lethal deterrence of crop-damaging wildlife, targeting real-world deployment in agricultural settings.

education

Robotics Education & Community Engagement

A community-engaged robotics programme linking coursework, hands-on student projects, and the SULAM outreach initiative — centred on robotic hand design and prototype development.

engineering infrastructure

Engineering Workshop Inventory & Infrastructure System

A low-cost, high-usability system for component categorisation, location tracking, and resource management in a high-mix engineering workshop environment.
